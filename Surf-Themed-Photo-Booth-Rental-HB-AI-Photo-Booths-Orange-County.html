What is a generative AI photo booth?

It is an advanced photo booth that uses artificial intelligence to instantly transform a person's photo into a new, stylized image. Instead of just adding a filter, it reimagines the photo in a chosen artistic style, such as a painting, a sketch, or even as a custom action figure.

A regular digital photo booth typically captures photos and may add digital props or static backgrounds. An AI photo booth uses generative artificial intelligence to completely reconstruct the photo in a new artistic style, offering a far more dynamic, creative, and personalized outcome.
